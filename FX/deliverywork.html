How does it work?

Our home delivery service is our chief innovation as it allows us to keep our overheads low and pass the savings onto you with the best rates in the market. It also means you can shop for foreign currencies from the comfort of your own home and receive them directly without having to organise collection.

Delivery is safe, secure and fully insured. It is available to any residential address in Australia and will arrive in 5 business days from when you place your order or on your nominated delivery date. The delivery cost is just $15.

Home delivery is available for orders of any value and as many different currencies and denominations as you need. Select the currencies you need in our online order portal and make payment via BPay or Bank Transfer (EFT) to our account.

Your order will be delivered by a secure courier service and won’t be released without the photo ID and signature of person who placed the order. This ensures maximum security for your currency order. If you miss your delivery, a communication card will be left in your letter box and we will try again the next business day.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does 'home delivery' work?

When you make your order, you’ll be asked to enter your residential delivery address which needs to match your drivers license. Proceed with the payment and completion of your order and receive your order within 5 business days. It needs to be received and signed for by the person who made the order with the matching drivers license.

2. Where in Australia is it available?

Any residential address in Australia.

3. How much does it cost?

Delivery cost is just $15. Our service has no fees or commissions and we offer the best rates in the market.

4. How much currency can I order?

Any amount up to A$10,000.

5. When will my order be delivered?

Your order will be delivered within 5 business days of order completion.

6. What if I’m not home or I miss my delivery?

If you miss your delivery, our courier will leave a communication card to let you know try to deliver again the next business day. If the delivery is missed a second time, a communication card will be left to let you know where you can pick up your currency (your local dispatch centre).

7. Can someone else receive the order on my behalf?

The person who receives the order must be the person on the drivers license used to make the order. The photo ID will be checked by the courier and the order will only be released to the correct party.
